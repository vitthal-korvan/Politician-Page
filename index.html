About Rahul Shewale

A civil engineer by training and a public representative by commitment – combining technical understanding with on-ground experience in Mumbai’s civic system.

Personal Background

Rahul Ramesh Shewale comes from a modest family with a strong service background. After completing his diploma in Civil Engineering from Government Polytechnic, Bandra, he chose public life as his path.

With roots in Mumbai and deep understanding of local challenges, his work focuses on practical solutions – better roads, safer housing and smoother everyday life for citizens.

  • Civil Engineering diploma – Govt. Polytechnic, Bandra
  • Ground connect with Mumbai South Central’s diverse communities
  • Guided by the vision of Balasaheb Thackeray & leadership of Eknath Shinde

Political Journey

Early Years

Entered civic politics as Corporator in Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC).

Standing Committee

Served four terms as Chairman of the powerful BMC Standing Committee, overseeing core civic projects.

2014 – 2024

Elected as Member of Parliament from Mumbai South Central, representing key areas like Dharavi, Sion and Chembur.

Shiv Sena (Shinde)

Part of Shiv Sena (Eknath Shinde) leadership, aligning development agenda with state-level priorities.

Core Focus Areas

Urban Infrastructure

Roads, bridges, storm-water drainage and public transport connectivity that match Mumbai’s scale.

Redevelopment & Housing

Safer, planned redevelopment for areas like Dharavi and old colonies with humane rehabilitation.

Basic Amenities

Water, sanitation, solid waste management and health facilities for everyday quality of life.

Youth & Community

Opportunities for youth, support for women and strong local community networks.
